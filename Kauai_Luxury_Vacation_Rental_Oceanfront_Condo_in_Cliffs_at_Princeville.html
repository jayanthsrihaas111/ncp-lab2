Welcome to The Cliffs at Princeville 7301!

The Cliffs at Princeville 7301 in Princeville, Kauai is an outstanding vacation rental condo bargain. Your dream trip to Hawaii has never been more affordable!

Details

The Cliffs at Princeville 7301 is a roomy 2-bedroom Kauai condo that encompasses over 1500 square feet, all dedicated to your comfort and enjoyment. The Cliffs at Princeville 7301 is located on Kauai's North Shore. Kauai is the most beautiful of the Hawaiian islands, and the North Shore is legendary for its towering mountains, lush plant life, and idyllic beaches. There is no more enchanting and relaxing vacation destination.

A virtual home away from home, this condo in Kauai's Princeville resort and golf community features the following:

  • a large, breezy living room overlooking the Pacific Ocean
  • a complete kitchen
  • a spacious master bedroom with a master bath
  • a loft bedroom with two double beds
  • another full bath
  • an ocean lanai that overlooks the ocean
  • a garden lanai with a view of Bali Hai

This unit is large enough to accommodate up to five adults.

Continuously remodeled and upgraded, this unit, one of the best in the Five-star Cliffs at Princeville, Kauai condo resort, has never been nicer!

Golfers' Paradise!

Guests staying at this Princeville vacation rental condo are entitled to a substantial discount at one of the most magnificent golf courses in the world. What a deal!
